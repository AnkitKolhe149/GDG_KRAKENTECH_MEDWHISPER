About MedWhisper

AI-Powered Early Detection for Silent Diseases

Our Mission

A large percentage of life-threatening diseases remain undiagnosed for years because symptoms are mild, ignored, or fragmented across reports. MedWhisper is on a mission to provide predictive prevention rather than reactive diagnosis.

We leverage advanced AI and machine learning to analyze comprehensive health data and identify disease risks early, before symptoms become severe or life-threatening.

What We Do

AI-Powered Analysis

Our advanced machine learning models analyze multiple health data points simultaneously to detect disease risks with high accuracy.

Multi-Disease Detection

We detect risks for diabetes, hypertension, cardiac disease, liver disorders, and mental health conditions in one platform.

Comprehensive Data Integration

We aggregate lab trends, lifestyle data, stress indicators, mental health patterns, and family history for holistic risk assessment.

Personalized Recommendations

Based on your risk profile, we provide actionable, personalized recommendations for prevention and early intervention.

Our Technology

MedWhisper is built on cutting-edge machine learning technologies:

  • LightGBM & XGBoost: Gradient boosting models for high-accuracy predictions
  • Random Forest: Ensemble methods for robust risk assessment
  • Firebase: Secure cloud authentication and real-time data storage
  • Modern Web Stack: Flask backend, Bootstrap UI, responsive design

Our Vision

We envision a world where silent diseases are detected early, where preventive medicine is personalized and data-driven, and where everyone has access to AI-powered health insights. Because early detection saves lives.
